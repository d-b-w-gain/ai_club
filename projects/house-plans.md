# House Plans Experiment

## Idea

Try using AI-assisted tools, and possibly CAD, to explore house plans or spatial layouts.

## Questions

- Can AI help with rough layouts or early thinking?
- Can it represent constraints clearly?
- Does it help a non-specialist think spatially?
- Where would professional design judgment still be needed?
