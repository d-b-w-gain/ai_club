# Eiffel Tower Rocket Animation

## Idea

A short AI-assisted 3D animation where a rocket tries to launch from the Eiffel Tower, or the Eiffel Tower itself becomes the rocket.

## Style

- Retro 1950s French New Wave cinema feel
- Black and white or restrained colour
- Film grain, practical-camera energy, and stylised framing
- Playful rather than polished

## Possible Tools

- AI image, video, writing, or planning tools
- Any practical 3D or animation workflow
- Existing models or CAD assets if useful
- Simple sound, narration, or editing experiments

## Next Step

Collect a few reference images, write a 30-second sequence, and make one small AI-assisted test shot.
